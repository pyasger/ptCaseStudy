The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structutes four feet off the ground. Each yurt has canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a roof dome that can be opened.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furished with a queen-size bed with down quilt and gas-fire stove. The luxury camping experiance also includes electricity and a sink with hot and cold running water. Shower and restroom facilities are located in the lodge.

What should i bring?

Bring a sence of adventure and some time to relax! Most guest also pack comfortable walking shoes and plan to dress for changing weather with layers of clothing.
